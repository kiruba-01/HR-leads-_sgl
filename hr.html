Users can buy the required number of credits in pay as you go on the pricing page.

A B2B contact detail has both an email and a phone number. 1 email = 1 credit 1 phone number = 1 credit. If the user wants both the email and phone number of the required B2B contact, the user has to pay 2 credits. If the user needs either an email or phone number, the user has to pay 1 credit for a B2B contact detail.

Credit is like a point or currency, used to get contact details of the leads. Whenever a user unlocks a lead, 1 credit shall be deducted.

HRLeads accepts both credit and debit card payments. It currently offers the following options Visa, Master Card, American Express, Discover & Diners, China union pay, Japan Credit Bureau (JCB), and Cartes Bancaires
